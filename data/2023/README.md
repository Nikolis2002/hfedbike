# data/2023/

CSVs here are gitignored. Expected contents: monthly Citi Bike trip
history CSVs for calendar year 2023, downloaded from
<https://citibikenyc.com/system-data>, plus the corresponding cleaned
OpenWeatherMap hourly table. These files are consumed by
`baseline/data_preparation/make_neighbs_2023/` and ultimately feed the
grid search in `baseline/model_search/`.
